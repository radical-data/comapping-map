Comapping Amelisweerd

About

What is Comapping Amelisweerd?

Comapping Amelisweerd looks at what mapping looks like when it is done from below, by the people who use and live in a space.

How can I participate?

By submitting 'data points' of pictures, audio, text or even walk routes, visitors to Amelisweerd fill up the online platform at comapping.org. These data points can focus on anything from where certain trees are to thoughts linked to different areas to just documenting everything yellow.

These points are shown at comapping.org, which brings these community observations together to explore interactively.

The piece will grow as the Gardening program progresses. Visitors can submit by themselves or in one of the datawalks organised by Creative Coding Utrecht.

Who created this?

The platform was designed and coded by Jo Kroese from Radical Data. It was co-created and funded by Creative Coding Utrecht.

What data should I submit?

Anyone visiting Amelisweerd is invited to record things of interest and submit them to the Comapping platform.
